Champions League Elo Ratings

A browser-based Elo dashboard for UEFA Champions League and European Cup matches. Ratings update chronologically, persist across seasons, and include controlled football-specific K-factors, goal-difference multipliers, and knockout bonuses.

Intended source openfootball/champions-league

Current build runs on illustrative normalized sample data, not a complete official match archive; the adapter layer is ready for football.txt imports.
